Restaurant Reviews

Madre Vegan Restaurant

This restaurant offers a wide range of plant-based dishes that are not only delicious but also organic. Highly recommended for its cozy ambiance and friendly staff.

Rating: 4.8/5

Bonboon Vegan Dessert Corner

Known for its incredible vegan desserts, Bonboon is a must-visit for anyone with a sweet tooth. The coconut-based ice cream is a fan favorite!

Rating: 4.5/5

Yemayá’s Vegan Corner

Offering traditional local vegan dishes, Yemayá’s is a great place for a quick meal with fresh ingredients and fast service.

Rating: 4.2/5

Studio Konijnenvoer

A modern eatery located in Breda, Studio Konijnenvoer is famous for its vegan sushi, fresh salads, and minimalist vibe. It's a perfect place for a healthy, delicious meal.

Rating: 4.7/5
