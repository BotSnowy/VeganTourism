Vegan Restaurants in the Netherlands

Discover some of the top vegan-friendly restaurants across the Netherlands

Cities Restaurants Description
Amsterdam Mr. & Mrs. Watson A stylish spot known for its creative plant-based dishes, including vegan cheese and comfort foods.
Rotterdam Gare du Nord A unique restaurant inside a vintage train carriage, serving a fully vegan, organic menu with seasonal ingredients.
Utrecht Oproer A microbrewery and restaurant offering delicious, innovative vegan meals paired with craft beers.
The Hague Veggies on Fire An eco-conscious eatery focused on organic, locally-sourced vegan dishes with a gourmet twist.
Maastricht Restaurant De Koperen Ster A cozy restaurant with a wide range of international vegan meals, from burgers to Buddha bowls.
Eindhoven The Happiness Café A vibrant café offering a variety of healthy vegan breakfasts, smoothies, and desserts.
