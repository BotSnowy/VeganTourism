Traveling as a vegan can be exciting and rewarding.

Amsterdam

Vegan Highlights: A bustling city with plenty of options for vegans. Enjoy creative plant-based menus at restaurants like "Vegan Junk Food Bar" or stop by "Vegabond" for a wide range of snacks and groceries. Must-Try: Vegan Dutch pancakes and Stroopwafels at local cafes.

Rotterdam

Use apps or guides to locate vegan restaurants or eateries that offer plant-based options.

Utrecht

Vegan Highlights: This historical city offers a cozy vibe with great vegan options like "SOY" and "Gys Utrecht." Must-Try: Vegan brunch at the canalside cafes.
